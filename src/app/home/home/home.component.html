About Us

Nagrath Aluminium Pvt. Ltd. designs, extrudes, builds, manufactures Aluminium profiles and systems for applications in various industries like Building & Construction, Automotive, Transportations and Clean Energy sectors. Our Vision is entirely established on make infrastructure Eco-smart by providing eco friendly profiles and solutions.

Our brand NALEX stands for the best quality driven products and echoes the green building concept for the new future. Production at NALEX is utterly driven by eco friendly methods. Thus, we are saving 95% energy by recycling aluminium and using it as raw material.

The government's thrust to increase indigenours production through green smart cities and Make in India campaign has boosted our vision of developing valued added products for both domestic and commercial application along with new innovative ideas and business opportunities. The idea of sustainable development is deeply ensconded in out business ethos and we strongly believe that Aluminium contributes to the basics of life.

WHY ALUMINIUM ?

RECYCLABLE

Aluminium is a very versatile & sustainable metal with infinite recycling capability.
ENERGY-SAVING

Producing Aluminium from recycled material saves 95% energy.

LOW-POLLUTION

Using recycled Aluminium instead of mining from bauxite reduced air pollution by 95% and water pollution by 97%

LONGLIFE

Withstands the damages caused to buildings by temperature, corrosion, humidity and warping

ENERGY-EFFICIENT

Extruded framed windows with thermal barriers are very effective in insulation of buildings and homes

AESTHETIC

HIGH PROPOSITION VALUE

Depleting rate of earth's natural resources is a major concern for unbalanced ecosystem. Aluminium, on other hand is accepted as the best alternative metal due to it abundance and recyclable qualities.
